Commands and Permissions
========================

Need to assert your dominance? Here's the list of ways you can boss around your players (and your configuration).

**The Meta Command**
--------------------
- ``/lpc reload``: Fixed a typo in the config? Run this to fix it without anyone noticing your shame. (Permission: ``lpc.reload``)

**Talking to People (or ignoring them)**
-----------------------------------------
- ``/msg <player> <message>``: Whisper sweet nothings (or insults) to another player. (Permission: ``lpc.msg``)
- ``/r <message>``: Reply to the last person who messaged you. Don't make them wait. (Permission: ``lpc.reply``)
- ``/ignore <player>``: The ultimate "talk to the hand". (Permission: ``lpc.ignore``)
- ``/socialspy``: Be a creep and read other people's private messages. (Permission: ``lpc.socialspy``)

**Moderation (The Fun Stuff)**
------------------------------
- ``/mute <player> [duration] [reason]``: Silence is golden. Make them experience it. (Permission: ``lpc.mute``)
- ``/unmute <player>``: Be a merciful god. (Permission: ``lpc.unmute``)
- ``/warn <player> <reason>``: A stern finger wag in digital form. (Permission: ``lpc.warn``)
- ``/warnings <player>``: Review their criminal record. (Permission: ``lpc.warnings``)
- ``/delwarn <id>``: Erase their sins. (Permission: ``lpc.delwarn``)

**The Rest**
------------
- ``/sc <message>``: Chat with other staff members. Usually used for deciding where to eat lunch. (Permission: ``lpc.staffchat``)
- ``/chatclear``: Wipe the slate clean. (Permission: ``lpc.chatclear``)
- ``/ch [channel] [message]``: Change your channel focus or shout into one. (Permission: ``lpc.channel.use``)
- ``/profile <player>``: Stalk their bio and infractions.
- ``/setbio <text>``: Tell the world how deep and mysterious you are.
- ``/slowmode <player> <seconds>``: Make them wait for it.
