==============================================
LPC – LuckPerms Chat Formatter (The Good One)
==============================================

Welcome to the documentation for LPC. Yes, we know, another chat plugin. But this one actually works, has null safety (thanks to Kotlin), and doesn't look like it was written in 2012.

LPC is a flexible chat formatting plugin with full MiniMessage support. It's designed specifically for use with LuckPerms, because why would you use anything else?

Stop squinting at ugly chat and start using something that respects your eyes.

LPC Reloaded is a **Pro plugin, but it's free on all platforms**. We deliver every single meaningful Pro feature without charging you a cent—simply **because we can**. We've licensed it under the **GPL**, because we believe in actual open source.

Why LPC? (The Anti-Bloat Promise)
---------------------------------

This project was forked from the original free version of LPC. Since then, we've reached **95% feature parity** with the "Pro" version through a strictly **clean-room implementation**. We didn't need to peek at their code to make something better; we just used our brains.

The remaining 5%? Those are the "features" we've explicitly refused to implement because they're stupid. 

While the `paid alternative <https://www.spigotmc.org/resources/122297/>`_ is busy charging you a monthly fee to bloat your server with features you never asked for, we're busy keeping things lean and functional. 

Here’s why LPC is better than the "Pro" version:

* **No "MiniMessage Native" Marketing:** We don't take credit for Paper's hard work. Advertising "Native MiniMessage" as a premium feature is like a car dealer advertising "Native Rubber" on the tires. Paper does the heavy lifting; we just use the API.
* **No "Grammar Systems":** We assume your players graduated from primary school. If they didn't, a plugin won't save them.
* **No "Reputation Systems":** We're a chat plugin, not a social credit simulator. We leave the dystopian nightmares to the "Pro" guys.
* **No "Custom Commands":** Because ``commands.yml`` actually works if you take 5 minutes to read the manual.
* **No "Staff Macros":** Because your staff should be talking to players, not spamming them with automated macros like a telemarketer.
* **No "AI Moderation":** We don't believe in sending your server's entire chat history to OpenAI just to figure out if someone said a bad word. Your data belongs to you, not Sam Altman.
* **No GUI Menus:** We believe you're smart enough to edit a text file. We won't make you waste your life clicking around a chest GUI like you're playing a minigame just to change a chat format.
